# Privacy

This is a demonstration application. **No real personal data is collected, stored or
transmitted.** Any details you enter (login, profile, team members) are written to
local JSON files inside the project for demo purposes only and are reset when the
seed data is restored.

- No real authentication — any email and password combination is accepted.
- No analytics, tracking, or third-party integrations are active.
- Not affiliated with Singtel Optus Pty Limited.

For the real Optus privacy policy, visit optus.com.au.
