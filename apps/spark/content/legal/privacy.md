**This is a demonstration app and is not affiliated with Spark New Zealand.** No real personal
information is collected, stored, or shared. The text below is placeholder content written for a
UI demo only.

## What this demo stores

This demo keeps a small amount of information in local JSON files on the machine running it:

- A demo account (name and email) used to show a signed-in experience.
- Illustrative usage, billing, plan, and add-on data.
- Preferences you toggle in Settings.

None of this data leaves the local environment, and any email or password is accepted at sign-in.

## What this demo does not do

- It does not connect to real Spark systems or any external service.
- It does not process real payments, SIMs, or roaming.
- It does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.

## Contact

Because this is a demo, there is no real privacy team to contact. For questions about the demo
itself, refer to the project README.
