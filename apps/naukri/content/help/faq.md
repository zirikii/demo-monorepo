# Help & FAQ

## How do I search for jobs?

Use the search bar on the home page or the Jobs page. Enter a keyword, choose your experience
level, and add a location, then hit **Search**. Refine results with the filters on the left.

## How do I apply to a job?

Open any job to view the full description, then click **Apply**. Confirm in the dialog and the
application will appear under **Applied** with its current status.

## How is my profile completeness calculated?

Completeness is based on the key sections of your profile — headline, skills, employment,
education, projects, certifications, and preferences. Filling more sections increases your
score and your visibility to recruiters.

## Is this the real Naukri.com?

No. This is an unofficial demo built to showcase a Naukri-style experience. All data is
dummy and stored locally.
