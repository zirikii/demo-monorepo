# Getting Started

Welcome to the Naukri demo! Here's how to make the most of it.

1. **Log in** with the demo credentials (any email/password works, or use the admin hint
   shown on the login page).
2. **Complete your profile** to improve recommendations and recruiter visibility.
3. **Search and filter jobs** on the Jobs page, then save or apply to roles you like.
4. **Track applications** under Applied, and respond to recruiter messages in your inbox.
5. **Explore Naukri 360** for simulated AI career tools.
