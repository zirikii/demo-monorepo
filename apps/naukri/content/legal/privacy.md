# Privacy Policy

> This is an unofficial demo application and is **not affiliated with Naukri.com or Info
> Edge (India) Ltd.** No real personal data is collected or processed. The text below is
> illustrative placeholder copy for the demo UI only.

## Information we "collect"

In this demo, all data is dummy and stored locally in JSON files on your machine. Nothing is
transmitted to any external server.

## How data is used

Profile, application, and preference data exist solely to demonstrate the product
experience. You may reset it at any time by restoring the seed files.

## Your choices

You can edit or remove any profile section from the Profile and Settings pages. Account
visibility to recruiters is simulated and configurable under Settings → Privacy.

## Contact

For questions about this demo, please refer to the project README.
