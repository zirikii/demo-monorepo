## Lead with impact

Recruiters spend seconds scanning each resume. Put your strongest, most relevant achievements near the top so they're impossible to miss.

## Quantify your achievements

Numbers tell a story. Instead of "improved performance", try "improved page-load performance by 40%, lifting conversion by 12%".

## Tailor for every role

Mirror the language in the job ad. If the role asks for "stakeholder management", make sure that phrase appears where it's genuinely true for you.

## Keep it clean and scannable

Use clear headings, consistent formatting and plenty of white space. A tidy layout signals attention to detail.

## Focus on outcomes, not duties

Anyone can list responsibilities. Show the difference you made and the value you delivered.

## Proofread, then proofread again

Typos undermine an otherwise great application. Read it aloud, and ask a friend to check it too.

## Make it easy to contact you

Include a current phone number and professional email. Keep your SEEK Profile up to date so hirers can reach you.
