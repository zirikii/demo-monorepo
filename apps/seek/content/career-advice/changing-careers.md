## Get clear on your why

Understanding what's driving the change helps you choose a direction that genuinely fits.

## Audit your transferable skills

You're not starting from zero. Communication, leadership, problem-solving and project skills travel across industries.

## Talk to people in the field

Informational chats with people already doing the work are the fastest way to learn what a role is really like.

## Close the gaps

Identify any skills or credentials you need, then build them through short courses, volunteering or side projects.

## Update your story

Reframe your resume and profile around your new direction, highlighting relevant, transferable experience.

## Take a confident first step

You don't have to leap all at once. A related role, contract or project can be a great bridge to your new career.
