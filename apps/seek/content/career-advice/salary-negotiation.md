## Do your research

Use salary guides and job ads to understand the market rate for your role, experience and location.

## Know your number

Decide on your target and your walk-away point before the conversation begins.

## Let them go first

Where you can, let the employer share a range first — it gives you valuable information.

## Sell the value

Frame your request around the value you bring, backed by concrete achievements.

## Consider the whole package

Salary is one part of the picture. Flexible work, leave, super, learning budgets and bonuses all add up.

## Stay positive and collaborative

Negotiation is a conversation, not a confrontation. Approach it with warmth and professionalism.
