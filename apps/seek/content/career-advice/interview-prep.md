## Research the company

Understand what the organisation does, who its customers are and what's happening in its industry. Reference specifics in your answers.

## Know your stories

Prepare a handful of concrete examples using the STAR method — Situation, Task, Action, Result — that show your impact.

## Prepare thoughtful questions

Great questions show genuine interest. Ask about the team, success in the role, and what the first 90 days look like.

## Practise out loud

Rehearsing your answers helps you sound natural and confident, not rehearsed.

## Sort the logistics

Test your tech for video interviews, plan your travel for in-person ones, and arrive a few minutes early.

## Follow up

A short, sincere thank-you note after the interview leaves a positive, lasting impression.
